01 / PROBLEM

When a boat capsizes 15 km from shore, who answers the call?

There is no answer. India's fishing communities operate beyond cellular range every single day — with no reliable way to send or receive emergency signals.

0

Existing solutions — VHF radios, satellite phones — cost ₹40,000–₹2,00,000 per unit. A fisherman earning ₹8,000 a month cannot afford this. TARANG costs ₹1,200.

02 / SOLUTION

TARANG — The network the sea doesn't have. Until now.

LoRa Mesh SOS Device

Works with zero cellular. Relays distress signals boat-to-boat-to-shore, up to 20 km offshore.

Auto-Anomaly Detection

Onboard AI flags capsizing and drift from motion + GPS. Triggers SOS automatically — even if no one presses a button.

AI Triage Agent (Groq + Mistral)

Shore-side dashboard prioritizes alerts by severity, weather, and distance. Recommends nearest rescue asset in real time.

Voice-First Multilingual Alerts

Kannada, Tulu, Malayalam, Hindi. Built for low-literacy users. No smartphone required.

Proactive Risk Broadcast

Pushes live cyclone and wave-height warnings to boats already at sea, before they hit danger.

₹1,200 Per Unit

Solar-rechargeable. No recurring data cost. Deployable by any fishing cooperative today.

04 / SYSTEM FLOW

Six steps. One saved life.

01
Track

The SOS device monitors GPS position and motion data continuously. No action needed from the fisherman.

02
Trigger

A capsize or drift anomaly is detected automatically by the IMU, or the fisherman presses the physical SOS button.

03
Relay

The distress packet — GPS coordinates, vessel ID, timestamp — hops boat-to-boat via LoRa mesh until it reaches a shore station.

04
Triage

The AI agent on the cloud scores severity: weather conditions, distance from shore, vessel type, anomaly type. Assigns the nearest responder.

05
Dispatch

The control-room dashboard displays the live alert on a Leaflet map. Operator confirms and dispatches Coast Guard or nearest vessel.

06
Alert

A multilingual voice call and SMS reaches the fisherman's family and nearby vessels. Status updates flow until rescue is confirmed.
